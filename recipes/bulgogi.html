Bulgogi

Description

Bulgogi is a Korean dish made of thinly sliced beef marinated in a sweet soy sauce and served with vegetables and rice. It's a popular dish in Korea and is often served at special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef tenderloin, top sirloin, or skirt steak), slice into ⅛-inch-thick slices
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 green onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds, coarsely ground
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Make marinade by combining the garlic, green onion, soy sauce, sugar, water, sesame oil, sesame seeds, and ground black pepper in a large bowl. Mix it well with a spoon until the sugar is well dissolved.
    2. '
  2. Add the sliced beef and mix everything by hand until the beef is covered in the marinade. Keep in the fridge at least 30 minutes.
  3. Cook on a pan or a grill, and transfer to a plate or a cast iron plate to serve with rice, lettuce, ssamjang, and more side dishes.
  4. To eat, wrap a spoonful of rice, a piece of bulgogi, and a little bit of ssamjang in a lettuce leaf. Fold it over and eat. Repeat!
